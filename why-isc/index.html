Why brokers and lenders switch

Six reasons mortgage teams move to ISC.

Different teams have different reasons. The pattern across clients who switched to ISC comes down to these six.

Reason 1

A dedicated rep who knows your file

When you call ISC, you reach the same support team every time. The person who picks up knows mortgage workflows, knows your account, and stays with your team. Most ISC clients can name their rep by first name.

→No ticket queues. No hold music. No round-robin routing.
Reason 2

Mortgage-focused since 1989

ISC has served only mortgage professionals for 37 years. Not auto lending. Not consumer credit. Not buy-now-pay-later. Every workflow, report format, and rep training is built specifically for mortgage origination.

→Your underwriter's edge cases are our normal cases.
Reason 3

Family-owned, long-horizon decisions

ISC is not owned by private equity. Not a roll-up. Not a publicly traded conglomerate. The same family has owned and operated ISC since founding. Decisions are made for long-term client relationships, not quarterly earnings calls.

→Stable ownership means consistent service, year after year.
Reason 4

Works inside your LOS

ISC integrates directly with the systems your team already uses. Encompass, BytePro, LendingPad, Calyx, MeridianLink, and many others. No new portal to learn. No extra logins. Reports, supplements, and rescores order from the LOS you already work in.

→Switching credit vendors does not mean changing how your team works.
Reason 5

One account, one invoice, one rep

Tri-merge reports, AUS soft pulls, SmartSelect, rapid rescores, supplements, verifications, score optimization. All from one vendor, one contract, one invoice, and one dedicated support team. Less time managing vendors and AP, more time closing loans.

→Consolidated billing simplifies reconciliation for ops and accounting.
Reason 6

Built on relationships, not transactions

Long-term relationships are earned through consistent service, responsive support, and people who know your account. Many of our client relationships span years, not months. Family ownership means the people you build the relationship with are still here.

→Built on long-term relationships, not transactions, since 1989.

FAQ

Frequently asked, before clients switch.

How long does it take to switch credit vendors?

For most mortgage brokers, account setup with ISC takes a few business days. For larger lenders with multi-branch setups or custom LOS integrations, onboarding typically runs one to two weeks. Your dedicated rep coordinates the entire process.

Will my existing LOS still work?

Most likely, yes. ISC integrates directly with 25+ loan origination systems including Encompass, BytePro, LendingPad, Calyx, and MeridianLink. Your team continues to work in the LOS they already use.

Do you require a long-term contract?

No. ISC does not require multi-year commitments to access standard credit products. Account terms are designed to be straightforward, and the relationship continues because clients choose to stay.

Will I have the same rep over time?

Yes. ISC assigns a dedicated support team to each account. Reps stay with their accounts long-term. Many of our client relationships span years.

What happens if I have an urgent file after hours?

Standard support runs business hours, Monday through Friday. For time-sensitive files, your rep provides direct contact information. Mortgage closings do not always cooperate with business hours, and ISC is set up to respond accordingly.

Can I order all my credit-related products through one account?

Yes. Mortgage credit reports, AUS soft pulls, SmartSelect, rapid rescores, credit supplements, borrower verifications, and score optimization tools all run through one ISC account with one invoice and one point of contact.

Is ISC compliant with FCRA and GLBA requirements?

Yes. ISC operates as a consumer reporting agency under the Fair Credit Reporting Act and adheres to GLBA requirements for consumer financial information. ISC has been operating under these frameworks since 1989.
