Our story

Founded on a simple belief.

ISC was founded in 1989 on a belief that credit reporting could be more personal, more responsive, and more dependable.

At the time, the industry was dominated by large, impersonal bureaus. The idea behind ISC was straightforward: mortgage professionals deserve a credit partner who picks up the phone, understands their account and helps keep loans moving, and actually helps them close, not just delivers a report and disappears.

Thirty-seven years later, that is still how we operate. The tools have changed. The belief has not.

Data & compliance

How we handle your data and your clients.

Every credit report ISC accesses is pulled for a specific borrower, for a specific purpose, at the request of an authorized client. That is not just our policy, it is the law under the FCRA and GLBA, and it is a standard we have maintained since 1989.

Borrower data is sensitive, and we treat it that way. ISC operates with strict access controls, TLS encryption end-to-end, and careful, compliant data handling.

If a borrower has questions about a report accessed through ISC, our Consumer Assistance team handles it directly, Consumer Assistance page or compliance@isccredit.com.
