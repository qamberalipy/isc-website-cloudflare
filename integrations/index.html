Getting connected, it takes minutes

1

Complete ISC onboarding

Following your onsite inspection (required by the credit bureaus), your ISC account is activated, typically within 1 business day. Your dedicated rep handles all onboarding paperwork and bureau credentialing.

2

Locate ISC in your LOS vendor directory

Most LOS platforms have ISC pre-configured as an approved credit reporting vendor. Search for "Information Searching Company" or "ISC Credit" in your vendor or integration directory.

3

Enter your ISC credentials

Your ISC support team provides your login credentials and walks you through any LOS-specific setup steps. This typically takes 5–15 minutes. Your rep stays on the call until your first test order is confirmed.

4

Place your first order and confirm delivery

Pull a test report directly from your LOS and confirm results are delivered correctly to the borrower file. Most integrations are fully live within minutes of credential entry.

What flows through the integration

Outbound from LOS

What ISC receives automatically

  • →Borrower name, SSN, date of birth
  • →Current and previous addresses
  • →Co-borrower information
  • →Report type selection
  • →Loan number for file association
Inbound to LOS

What ISC delivers back automatically

  • →Full credit report PDF to the borrower file
  • →Tri-bureau scores (FICO, VantageScore 4.0)
  • →MISMO-formatted data for DU/LP submission
  • →Supplement updates to existing files
  • →Verification results (VOI, VOE, flood, SSA-89)
